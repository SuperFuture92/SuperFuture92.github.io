Our Mission

Further the Knowledge sharing campaign will be a leader in preparing professionals who provide leadership and exemplary educational and related services to improve the lives of individuals in a changing and complex global society.

Our Vision

Empower Africans to become outstanding educators, scholars, and researchers, to advance in the profession of education, as broadly defined, through research on the science and art of teaching and learning, the effective uses of technology, and the analysis and development of leadership and educational policy.
